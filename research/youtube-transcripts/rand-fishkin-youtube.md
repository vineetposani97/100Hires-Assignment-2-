## Video: Demand Generation vs Lead Generation

### Core Idea
Demand generation creates awareness before capturing leads.

### Key Insights
- Most buyers don’t convert immediately
- Brand familiarity drives future conversions
- SEO alone is not enough anymore
- Dark social plays a huge role

### Key Takeaways
- Invest in brand building
- Create valuable content consistently
- Think long-term, not short-term leads

### Why This Matters (B2B SaaS Context)
- Builds inbound demand over time
- Reduces reliance on paid ads
- Improves conversion efficiency
