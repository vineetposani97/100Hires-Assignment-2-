## Video: Product Growth Frameworks

### Core Idea
Growth is driven by strong product fundamentals, not hacks.

### Key Insights
- Retention is the biggest growth driver
- Product-market fit is everything
- Growth loops outperform funnels
- Small improvements compound

### Key Takeaways
- Focus on user experience first
- Build habit-forming products
- Measure what matters (retention, engagement)

### Why This Matters (B2B SaaS Context)
- Sustainable growth comes from product strength
- Reduces reliance on paid acquisition
- Improves long-term scalability
