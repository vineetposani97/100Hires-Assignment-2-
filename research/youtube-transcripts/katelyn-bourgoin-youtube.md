## Video: Why People Buy (Customer Psychology)

### Core Idea
People don’t buy logically — they buy based on psychological triggers and emotional drivers.

### Key Insights
- Buyers want progress, not products
- Pain points drive action more than features
- Clarity beats cleverness in messaging
- Understanding customer language is critical

### Key Takeaways
- Focus on customer struggles, not product features
- Use customer research to guide messaging
- Make value obvious and immediate

### Why This Matters (B2B SaaS Context)
- Improves conversion rates
- Strengthens positioning
- Makes marketing more persuasive and effective
