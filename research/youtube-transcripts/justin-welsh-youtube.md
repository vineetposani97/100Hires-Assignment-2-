## Video: How to Build a One-Person Business

### Core Idea
You don’t need a big team to build a successful business — leverage and systems matter more.

### Key Insights
- Build once, sell many times
- Audience-first approach works best
- Simplicity scales better than complexity
- Focus on distribution, not just creation

### Key Takeaways
- Create digital products
- Build an audience on LinkedIn
- Focus on systems, not effort

### Why This Matters (B2B SaaS Context)
- Small teams can compete with larger players
- Content becomes a distribution engine
- Scalable growth without heavy hiring
