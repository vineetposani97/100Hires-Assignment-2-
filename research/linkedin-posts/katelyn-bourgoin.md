## Post 1: Ownable Idea Method

Link - https://www.linkedin.com/posts/katebour_your-buyer-already-knows-what-they-want-activity-7437471497369161728-M_7n?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAABCd18UBZhw_DC1Kh6aT2qHRjLe9bqO1Ej4

**Key Idea:**
Strong positioning comes from owning a unique idea in the customer’s mind.

**Summary:**
- Excavating customer beliefs and problems
- Distilling a clear, ownable argument
- Claiming a unique position
- Shaping messaging around it
- Activating through content strategy

**Why this matters for B2B SaaS:**
- Helps stand out in crowded markets
- Creates strong brand positioning
- Makes messaging clearer and more memorable

## Post 2: Focus on What Won’t Change

Link - https://www.linkedin.com/posts/katebour_dont-ask-how-can-i-survive-all-this-change-activity-7440731901910269952-7qbm?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAABCd18UBZhw_DC1Kh6aT2qHRjLe9bqO1Ej4

**Key Idea:**
Instead of chasing trends, focus on timeless customer needs.

**Summary:**
- Jeff Bezos focused on what won’t change:
- Lower prices
- More selection
- Faster delivery
- Customers still want trust and reliability
- Noise increases, but fundamentals remain

**Why this matters for B2B SaaS:**
- Prevents chasing every new trend
- Keeps strategy grounded in customer behavior
- Builds long-term, sustainable growth
